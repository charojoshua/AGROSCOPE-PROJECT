About Us

Agro scope is an agricultural based industry that provides a wide range of agro services in both animals and crops. Located in kampala Uganda 6th street industrial area It was started by 5 agriculturalists in 2018 till date We deal in providing consultancy services in any field or area both for livestock or crops We also provide farming modern tools( tractors, combines harvester and many others) Agro scope is a non-profitable industry started with the aim of improving farming in many African countries but most especially EAST AFRICA


AIMS

Providing information about cultivation of the soil for the growing of crops and the rearing of animals to provide food, wool, and other products

Provision of raw materials for agro-allied industries

Provision of market for industrial goods

Providing guidance to the farmers

Providing farm equipment to the farmers
